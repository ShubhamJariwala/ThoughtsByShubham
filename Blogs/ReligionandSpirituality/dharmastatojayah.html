Where There is Dharma and Righteousness There is Victory

यतः कृष्णस्ततो धर्मो यतो धर्मस्ततो जयः

We got the entire learnings from Shree Mad Bhagwad Gita, but generally when we pray to God we ask for resources, wealth and Material gains.
Giving a thought to the one incident, we should remenber that God gave Resources to Duryodhan even, Shree Krishna gave his entire army to Duryodhan. Where as Arjun only prayed for the Company of Shree Krishna.
Shree Krishna asked Arjun, is he sure of choosing Shree Krishna over his army, and also Shree Krishna wouldn't be taking any part in the war of Kurukshetra. At that time Arjun replied to Shree Krishna, "यतः कृष्णस्ततो धर्मो यतो धर्मस्ततो जयः", which means where there is Krishna, there is Dharma, and where there is Dharma there will be Victory.
Eventhough Shree Krishna didnt take part directly in the war, he was a 'Saarathi', companion of Arjun and helped him, in different aspects of the war. It was only due to the companionship of Lord Shree Krishna, Pandavs were able to achieve the victory.

This teaches us that, should always focus on doing right things, be righteous, and always pray to God to do good to us in a way that he wishes. Always believe, Gods Plan will always be greater and more beautiful than all your disappointments, Gods Plan is always the best.
