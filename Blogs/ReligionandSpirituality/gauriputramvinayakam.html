Gauriputram Vinayakam

वक्रतुण्ड महाकाय सूर्यकोटि समप्रभ ।
निर्विघ्नं कुरु मे देव सर्वकार्येषु सर्वदा ॥

Ganeshji is refered to Vighnaharta, which means someone who removes all the obstacles in the process of achieving a goal. At the start of any Pooja or any auspicious ceremony we first of all Devi/Devtas and Gods we do the Prayer of Ganpati
Ganeshji is the son of Lord Shiv and Ma Parvati, who is the form of Ma Amba/Adhyashakti, therefore Ganeshji is also referred to as Gauriputram.
Shivji, the father of Ganeshji is known for being calm and destroyer for all the bad qualities within the person. Shivji is the epitome of 'Dhyan', which demands highest form of stability.
Ma Parvati who is a form of Ma Amba/Adhyashakti is believed to be the energy of entire universe. Ma Amba is know for giving the strengthnas well as bravery in every circumstances of life.
Ganeshji is the combination of energies of both Shivji as well as Ma Parvati. Ganeshji provides stability, removes bad qualities as well as provides the strength and bravery to complete anything that we desire to do.
This is why Ganeshji is prayed first of all, at the start of anything.
Ganeshji also governs Muldhara Chakra in the body. The Stability of Muldhara Chakra is very important for the stability of mind.
May Ganeshji provide stability to your mind and removes all the obstaces from your life.
